Pasta al Ragú

a pasta dish served with a crumbled meat sauce drapped over a bed of pasta.

1 ¾ oz butter
1 ¾ oz minced prosciutto or pancetta
1 large carrot, finely chopped
1 celery stalk, finely chopped
1 onion, finely chopped
3 ½ oz minced lean veal or beef
3 ½ oz minced lean pork
1 glass of dry red wine
A little beef or chicken stock
3 tbsp tomato paste
Salt and pepper
