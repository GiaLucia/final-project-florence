10. Santa Croce

This church is the largest Franciscan church in Italy. The church is known for holding the tombs of Michelangelo and Dante Alighieri, both native Florentines. This beautiful church has stained glass windows and frescoes that give the church its true character. The outside area is a vast open space where people go to hang out on the front stairs of the church, play soccer and shop at eh leather stores that surround the church.
