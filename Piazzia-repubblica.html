7. Piazza della Repubblica

It was built at the end of the 19th centuary as the historical center of Florence. This Piazza is famous for its the triumphal arch, “Arcone”. This Piazza during the day is very popular for people to sit and soak up the sun or to just walk around. The night time there are many cafes that turn into lounges and nightclubs that are outdoors.
