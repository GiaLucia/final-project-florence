What to Eat

There was never a meal in Italy that I didn't like. The food is always fresh and has no added preservatives and fats. There are a few dishes that are famous to the area and I consider a must eat!

Pasta al Ragu

a pasta dish served with a crumbled meat sauce drapped over a bed of pasta

Spaghetti Carbonara

Pancetta (Italian style bacon) with scrambled eggs mixed in severed over a bed of spaghetti.

Bistecca Fiorentina

Famous dish in Florence a T-bone steak only cooked 7 minutes on each side.

Ribollita

A heavy stew that has a Barley salad, pappa con pomodoro (bread soaked with tomatoes)
