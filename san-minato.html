6. San Miniato al Monte

This church is setteled on the top of a hill facing the city of Florence. The view from the church is breath taking. There seem to be an endless number of stairs that you have to climb inorder to reach the top. The Church was built over the grave of a famous Christian Myrtr Saint Minias. He was of Armenina decent and some say he was of royality. He was decapitacted. The church started to be built in 1018 and it took over 200 years to complete it.
