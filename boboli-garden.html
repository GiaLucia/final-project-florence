5. Giardini di Boboli

This beautiful garden was started back in the 16th century by the Medici Family who ran Florence for many years. The garden is located in the back of Pitti Palace. The garden was not open to the public until 1776. There are many fountaisn and statues that you can sit next to and enjoy the beauty of this garden.
