8. Piazza Michelangelo

This Piazza is located only a few steps from San Miniato. Giuseppe Poggi a Florentine architect created this Piazza in 1860. This Piazza is named after the famous Michelangelo Buonarroti. In his honor there is a bronze statue of his famous David sculpture. The Statue now green in color stands tall over the Piazza and over looks the city. This Piazza is known for its amazing panoramic view of the Duomo and Ponte Vecchio. Becaue it is on a hill there is nothing that obstructs your view.
