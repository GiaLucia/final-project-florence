2. The David

It can be found in the Galleria dell’Accademia. It was sculptured by Michelangelo in 1501-1504. This 17 foot tall statue was carved 1 block of marble. The statue is amazing with Michelangelo’s attention to detail and the human anatomy. Be careful when viewing the statue you are not allowed to take pictures!
